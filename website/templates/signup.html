Terms and Conditions
1. Purpose

This system allows parishioners to request official church documents — Baptismal, Confirmation, Wedding, and Death Certificates — and helps parish staff manage and process these requests efficiently.

2. Account and Authentication
  • You must register an account and verify your identity using your preferred authentication method (e.g., fingerprint, PIN, or USB key).
  • You are responsible for keeping your account and authentication device secure.
3. Data Privacy
  • Only necessary personal information is collected to fulfill requests.
  • Data access is restricted to authorized users only.
  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information through the Parish Office.
4. Using the System
  • Submit document requests through the online form.
  • Choose a preferred pickup date during document release days: Wednesday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM (with a lunch break from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM).
  • Check the website to confirm when your document is ready before going to the Parish Office.
  • All documents must be picked up in person. Documents can not be sent through digital methods.
5. Acceptable Use
  • Provide accurate and truthful information.
  • Keep login credentials and devices secure.
  • Use the system only for its intended purpose.
  • Report any issues or suspicious activity immediately.
6. Limitations
  • Only Baptismal, Confirmation, Wedding, and Death certificates are supported.
  • Older physical records must be manually added to the system.
  • Pickup is only available on the official release days and times.
7. Suspension and Termination

The Parish Office may suspend or terminate your access for violations such as system misuse, unauthorized access attempts, or data tampering.

8. Updates and Contact

Terms may change over time. Continued use indicates your agreement to the latest version.
