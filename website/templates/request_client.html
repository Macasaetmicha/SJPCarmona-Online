Steps in Requesting Certificates

To submit a request, please have the following information ready:

  • Full name of the document holder
  • The type of ceremony certificate to request (e.g., Baptism, Marriage)
  • The date of the ceremony (at least the year)

Processing usually takes 3-5 business days.

Note: Requests can only be canceled within 24 hours from when request was made.
